First, food!

Yampa

Yampa is our very own McDonalds - we said what we said! Veg Sandwich, French fries and Oreo Milkshake is all you need to make your day on campus. Things to try: Samosa bun, Oreo milkshake & French fries It's always glass half full with Yampa's chai, THE BEST.

LKB

If Yampa is McD then LKB is Burger King, it's got some good stuff Things to try: Cutlet Sandwich, Veg Maggie

Mylari

Mylari is the direct way to the heart and your stomach. Chinese, North Indian, South Indian, Chats, Cool Drinks and the list is endless. Mylari's Chai is worth every sip, Chai peelo friends! If you get a chair and table with your friends at Mylari, it's a good day for you.

Lara’s Cafe

From tea to coffee, from chilli paneer to samosas, from hangry to happy! Lara’s Cafe is the newest addition to the list of hangout spots on campus, and you’re quite literally in for a treat. Situated right next to Yampa and LK Burgers, Lara’s Cafe has a wide range of items on its menu and its own seating area. After a long day of classes, Lara is the go to spot!

Kannan Bakery

The world-famous bakery, according to its owner at least, is the place to go for fresh hot rolls, burgers, puffs and amazing cold fruit juices. Don't miss out on their thick mango shake.

Fun places to hangout

CS Lawn

A pretty chill place to lie down during hot summers

MBA Block

With a beautiful garden at its entrance, the MBA block holds several event in its amphi-theater.

Mech Auditorium

The auditorium is always brimming with dance, drama, song, and fun events. Post college class hours, it is an area of creativity and letting go of stress. Located behind the administration block, it is the hub for cultural practices.

Football Steps/Cricket Ground

Bring your dabbas and your friend here, so many cricket matches - so many self-proclaimed umpires, so many conversations.

BB Court

Unsuccessful Goa plans, hot gossip, fun times with homies - feels like home, EVERY SINGLE TIME.

Gym

Fitness freaks- worry not, we not only have Yampaaa but also a freaking 'GYM' paaa. ( Pun intended) Go to the Gym accurately at 4 pm for all gym-related queries.

Parking Lot

Temporary Home not only to our gaadis, but also to our endless conversations.

Our college doggus

We have the cutest and most precious paws here in college. Cuddling them and feeding doggus with biscuits are a must, or else wooof woooff.

These are just some of the many fun spots to hangout, explore the rest on your own!
