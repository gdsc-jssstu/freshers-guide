Quick Reminder

Department offices are not always next to the classrooms!

Wifi and 2Ps

A detailed guide to get your wifi credentials - 1.Fill out the wifi form 2.Where do you get your wifi form? Ah, our provider, MCV! Fill in the details and get a signature from your HOD. 3.Next, find the wifi guy to get your credentials 4.Here’s a thing about the wifi guy; no one knows where he is :) You can still find your missing pair of socks that went missing 2 years ago, but finding the wifi guy is just impossible 5.Make sure to get your wifi credentials with enough persistence and patience, that is all you need to get your wifi access!

Reference Section

This can be your space that has amazing wifi and peace. You have the freedom to bring your laptop, books and concentration to the Reference Section.

Supercomputer

CS dept has its very own supercomputer which can run your high-speed computations! You can utilize it for any project of your choice; climate research, quantum mechanics or use it to make a meme (if you get the permission of course :p )

Xerox Centre

MCV is your provider for printed notes (one day before the internals :”) ), college forms and all your photocopy requirements. They already have copies of printed notes, all you need to do is collect them!

Stationery Shops

There is always scope of forgetting your scientific calculator or A4 size sheets or your basic stationery, don't worry, our college Stationery shop is here for your rescue. Also, it has a huge fish tank at the centre, do say ‘Hi’ to the fishes

Punjab National Bank AKA PNB

This is your go-to place for all your bank-related work. Lucky charm if you find them open anywhere near their “lunch hours”.

SJCE Co-operative Society

Your Genie for Record books, Blue books, Lab manuals etc. Don't commit the mistake of entering this place right before internals, you’ll have a hard time. Get your bluebooks in advance. Also, they only accept cash, no Gpay, no Phonepe; yes we live in the 21st century.

Library

Do you like doing things the old school way and reading big fat books instead of scrolling through PPTs? A library card is all you need. Your Fee receipt and two passport size photographs are all you need to get your library card done. Apart from academic books you can also find some really interesting books at the very end of the library, do explore it ;)

Quadrangle

All your flag hoisting happens here. We have a World war period aircraft on display too!
